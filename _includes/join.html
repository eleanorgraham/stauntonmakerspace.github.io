Dual Citizenship / Unique Requests

If you want membership at both the Makerspace and the Co-Working space, just sign up as a “Dweller” and let us know. We can accommodate other combinations as well.

If you do not want to become a full member, but you would like to support the Makerspace, join our members only mailing list, and receive invitations to events, you can set up a subscription as a supporter for $25 a month. Supports are always welcome to drop by and learn more about current projects and crazy ideas.
